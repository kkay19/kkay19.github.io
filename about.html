About Me

Heya! I'm Kaykay, aside from being known in real life as Phạm Đăng Khoa (Vietnamese), I'm also called by many names. I am currently a middle school student. I draw bears, bunnies and cats. That means I usually turn the scary-looking animatronic Freddy Fazbear into something cute, something that isn't your nightmare. Where do I sit to draw those? My classroom. I learn all sorts of stuff, but the most frequent subject that would pop up throughout the week is Advanced English. I'm really competitive, always signing up for competitions available to me, because I know it would help me appear on the big screen.
